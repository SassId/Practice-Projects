Materia

In Final Fantasy VII Remake, materia plays a pivotal role in combat and customization. These magical orbs imbue characters with spells, abilities, and enhancements. Equipping materia grants unique powers, such as elemental spells or support abilities, shaping each character's combat style. As characters progress, they gain access to more materia slots, deepening customization options and strategic possibilities in battles.

Limit Breaks

In Final Fantasy VII Remake, limit breaks are powerful abilities unique to each character that become available during battles when the limit gauge is filled. These devastating moves deal significant damage or provide crucial support, turning the tide of battles in the player's favor. As characters grow stronger, they unlock new and more potent limit breaks, adding depth and excitement to combat encounters throughout the game.

Weapons

In Final Fantasy VII Remake, weapons play a crucial role as indispensable tools for characters, providing not only distinctive abilities but also diverse attributes that profoundly influence their combat style and prowess. As characters progress and acquire new weapons, they unveil a treasure trove of additional skills and upgrades, enriching strategic customization and empowering them to adeptly adapt to an array of challenges encountered throughout the game's enthralling journey.
